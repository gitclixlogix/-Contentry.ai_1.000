## Long Description (3,000 characters max)

**Transform Your Outlook Email Communication with AI-Powered Content Analysis**

Contentry.ai brings enterprise-grade content compliance and quality analysis directly into Microsoft Outlook. Ensure every email you send is on-brand, culturally appropriate, and factually accurate—before you hit send.

**Key Features:**

• **3-Pillar Analysis** - Score every email on Compliance, Cultural Sensitivity, and Accuracy
• **Real-Time Feedback** - See scores update as you type
• **Strategic Profiles** - Configure multiple "Brand Brains" for different communication contexts
• **Actionable Recommendations** - Get specific suggestions, not just scores
• **Dashboard Integration** - Track all analyses in your Contentry.ai account

**Privacy & Security:**

• GDPR compliant (Norwegian company)
• Email content processed securely, never stored permanently
• Enterprise-grade encryption

**Perfect For:**

• Sales teams sending proposals
• HR departments crafting communications
• Marketing ensuring brand consistency
• Legal and compliance professionals
• Customer success teams

**Supported Platforms:**

• Outlook on the Web
• Outlook Desktop (Windows & Mac)

**Getting Started:**

1. Install from AppSource
2. Open Outlook and compose a new email
3. Click "Analyze Email" in the ribbon
4. Sign in with your Contentry.ai account
5. Write with confidence as AI guides your communication
