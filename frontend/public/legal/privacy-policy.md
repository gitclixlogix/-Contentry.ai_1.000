## 5. Content Analysis and AI Processing

### 5.1 How Content is Processed
- Content submitted for analysis is processed by our AI systems
- Analysis results are generated in real-time and stored in your account history
- We use third-party AI providers (e.g., OpenAI, Anthropic) to power our analysis

### 5.2 Data Retention for Content
- Analyzed content and results are retained in your account for your reference
- You can delete your content history at any time
- Upon account deletion, all associated content is permanently removed within 30 days

### 5.3 Third-Party AI Providers
- Content sent to AI providers is processed according to their data processing agreements
- We have Data Processing Agreements (DPAs) in place with all AI providers
- AI providers are prohibited from using your content to train their models

## 12. Browser Extension and Email Add-ins

### 12.1 Browser Extension
- Accesses text content only in active text fields you are editing
- Does not track browsing history or collect data from other websites
- Stores authentication tokens locally in your browser

### 12.2 Gmail Add-on
- Accesses email subject and body only during active composition
- Does not read your inbox or sent emails
- Requires explicit user action to analyze content

### 12.3 Outlook Add-in
- Accesses email content only in compose mode
- Does not access calendar, contacts, or other Outlook data
- Requires user to initiate analysis
