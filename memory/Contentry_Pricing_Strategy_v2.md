# Executive Summary

This document outlines Contentry.ai's innovative **hybrid credit-based pricing strategy** designed to align costs with actual value delivered while maintaining predictability for users. Our model combines the best of fixed and usage-based pricing with intelligent caps to ensure fairness, transparency, and sustainable margins.

## Key Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Value-Aligned** | Credit costs reflect actual computational resources and value delivered |
| **Transparent** | Users see estimated costs before actions and actual costs after |
| **Predictable** | Caps prevent bill shock while allowing flexibility |
| **Fair** | Simple tasks cost less; complex tasks cost more (but are capped) |
| **Scalable** | Model works from individual creators to enterprise teams |
