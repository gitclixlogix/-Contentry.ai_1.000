This is a brand guidelines document for Contentry AI.

Our brand voice should be:
- Professional but approachable
- Informative and helpful
- Innovative and forward-thinking

Key messages:
1. AI-powered content creation saves time and improves quality
2. Our platform helps businesses maintain brand consistency
3. We prioritize user privacy and data security

Target audience:
- Marketing professionals
- Content creators
- Social media managers
- Small business owners

Do not mention:
- Competitor products by name
- Pricing details in promotional content
- Unrealistic promises about AI capabilities
