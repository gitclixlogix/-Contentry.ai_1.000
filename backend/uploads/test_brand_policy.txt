BRAND POLICY GUIDELINES

1. PROHIBITED CONTENT:
   - No political statements or opinions
   - No mentions of competitor products
   - No negative language or criticism
   - No discussion of pricing or discounts outside official campaigns

2. BRAND VOICE:
   - Always positive and encouraging
   - Professional but friendly tone
   - Focus on customer value and benefits

3. TOPICS TO AVOID:
   - Religion and politics
   - Controversial social issues
   - Personal opinions on sensitive topics

4. REQUIRED ELEMENTS:
   - Always use brand hashtags: #OurBrand #Innovation
   - Maintain consistent brand terminology
