COMPANY BRAND POLICY DOCUMENT

1. BRAND VOICE AND TONE
- Always maintain a professional and respectful tone
- Avoid negative language about competitors
- Use inclusive language that welcomes all audiences

2. CONTENT GUIDELINES
- No political statements or controversial opinions
- Avoid inflammatory language or hate speech
- Focus on positive brand messaging

3. SOCIAL MEDIA STANDARDS
- All posts must align with company values
- No personal attacks or aggressive language
- Maintain consistency across all platforms

4. PROHIBITED CONTENT
- Discriminatory language based on race, gender, religion
- Offensive or inappropriate humor
- Negative commentary about competitors or industry peers

This document serves as a guideline for all content creation and moderation activities.
