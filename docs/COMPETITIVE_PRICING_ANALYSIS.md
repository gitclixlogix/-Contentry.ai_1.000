## Competitive Advantages for Contentry

### Price Advantages
1. **Approval Workflows at $149/mo** - Competitors require Enterprise ($1K+/mo)
2. **Team Features at $149/mo** - Lower than Copy.ai Agents ($249/mo)
3. **API Access at $149/mo** - Lower than most competitors

### Feature Advantages
1. **Built-in compliance checking** - Unique in market
2. **3-tier knowledge system** - More sophisticated than competitors
3. **Integrated approval workflows** - Not available at any price from most competitors
4. **Browser extension** - In-context assistance

### Gap Analysis
| Gap | Opportunity |
|-----|-------------|
| No competitor offers approval at mid-tier | Lead with compliance/governance messaging |
| SSO typically $1K+/mo minimum | Consider including at Professional tier as differentiator |
| Most focus on generation only | Position as full content lifecycle platform |
