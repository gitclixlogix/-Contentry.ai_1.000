## 2. Representative Case Study: Enterprise Communications Team

### 2.1 Company Profile

**Industry:** Financial Services  
**Size:** 5,000+ employees  
**Challenge:** Managing communications during regulatory changes

### 2.2 Situation

- 15-person communications team
- Multiple brands and regions
- Strict compliance requirements
- Slow approval cycles (5-7 days average)
- Inconsistent messaging across channels

### 2.3 Solution Implementation

**Phase 1: Setup (Week 1)**
- Connected Azure AD for SSO
- Created 5 Strategic Profiles (one per brand)
- Uploaded compliance guidelines to knowledge base

**Phase 2: Workflow Design (Week 2)**
- Configured 3-stage approval: Creator → Legal → Director
- Set up compliance scoring thresholds
- Trained team on platform

**Phase 3: Rollout (Week 3-4)**
- Piloted with internal communications
- Expanded to external communications
- Integrated with LinkedIn publishing

### 2.4 Results

| Metric | Before | After | Improvement |
|--------|--------|-------|-------------|
| Approval cycle time | 5.2 days | 1.8 days | 65% faster |
| Compliance issues found pre-publication | 0% | 18% | Risk prevented |
| Content production | 12 pieces/week | 45 pieces/week | 3.75x increase |
| Brand voice consistency score | 62% | 89% | 44% improvement |

### 2.5 Key Quote (Representative)

> "Before Contentry, we caught compliance issues after publication. Now we catch them before anyone sees them. That's the difference between a PR crisis and a normal Tuesday."
> — *Communications Director*

## 3. Representative Case Study: HR/Talent Management Company

### 3.1 Company Profile

**Industry:** HR Services / Staffing  
**Size:** 2,500 employees  
**Challenge:** Managing employer brand during M&A integration

### 3.2 Situation

- Recently acquired competitor
- Two distinct company cultures merging
- Employee concerns about job security
- Need for consistent, sensitive communications
- Multiple stakeholders (HR, Legal, Leadership)

### 3.3 Solution Implementation

**Strategic Profile Setup:**
- Created "Integration Communications" profile
- Defined tone: Empathetic, transparent, professional
- Uploaded: Integration FAQ, approved messaging, legal guidelines

**Workflow Configuration:**
- 4-stage approval: HR Draft → Legal Review → Leadership → CEO Final
- Cultural sensitivity scoring enabled for global workforce
- Scheduling for coordinated announcements

### 3.4 Results

| Metric | Outcome |
|--------|--------|
| Communications produced | 47 pieces over 3 months |
| Compliance issues caught | 12 (26% of drafts) |
| Cultural sensitivity flags | 8 adaptations made |
| Employee sentiment | Maintained stable (no crisis) |
| Time to produce comms | Reduced from 3 days to 4 hours |

### 3.5 Key Quote (Representative)

> "During an acquisition, every word matters. Contentry helped us get the words right the first time, and gave Legal peace of mind that nothing would slip through."

### 4.1 Company Profile

**Industry:** Manufacturing

| Efficiency | Approval cycle time | Days from draft to approved | 50-70% reduction |

| Quality | Cultural sensitivity score | Average score | 80+ average |
| Risk | Violations caught pre-pub | Count | 15-25% of content |
| Risk | Post-publication corrections | Count | 80%+ reduction |

### 6.2 Qualitative Metrics

| Category | Metric | Assessment Method |
|----------|--------|------------------|
| Satisfaction | User satisfaction | Survey/NPS |
| Adoption | Feature utilization | Usage analytics |
| Confidence | Team confidence in content | Qualitative feedback |
| Alignment | Stakeholder alignment | Approval rate trends |

### 6.3 ROI Calculation Framework

```
Time Savings:
- Hours saved per content piece × pieces per month × hourly rate
- Example: 2 hours × 100 pieces × $75/hour = $15,000/month

Risk Avoidance:
- Estimated cost of compliance violation × probability × pieces
- Example: $50,000 × 5% × 100 pieces = $250,000 avoided

Efficiency Gains:
- Approval cycle reduction × value of faster time-to-market
- Example: 3 days saved × $500/day opportunity cost = $1,500/piece
```
