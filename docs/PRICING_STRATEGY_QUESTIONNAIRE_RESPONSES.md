# SECTION 1: PRODUCT & VALUE PROPOSITION

## 1.1 Core Product Overview

**Q1: What is Contentry.ai's primary value proposition?**
> Contentry.ai is an AI-powered content intelligence platform that enables businesses and creators to generate, analyze, moderate, and optimize professional content at scale while ensuring brand compliance and regulatory adherence.

**Q2: What are the 3-5 most important features/capabilities of Contentry.ai?**
> 1. **AI Content Generation & Analysis** - GPT-powered content creation with compliance checking, promotional content detection, and quality scoring
> 2. **Content Moderation & Approval Workflow** - Multi-tier approval system with role-based permissions for enterprise teams
> 3. **Strategic Profiles & Knowledge Base** - Brand voice management with RAG-enabled knowledge retrieval for consistent messaging
> 4. **Multi-Platform Social Media Management** - Schedule, publish, and analyze content across LinkedIn, Twitter, and other platforms via Ayrshare integration
> 5. **Enterprise-Grade Security & Compliance** - SSO (Okta, Microsoft), role-based access control, multi-tenant data isolation, and GDPR compliance

**Q3: What is the core value metric?**
> **Primary:** AI Credits (content generations, analyses, image creations)
> **Secondary:** Number of users/seats, social profiles connected, storage used
> The platform uses a credit-based system where different AI operations consume varying credit amounts.

**Q4: How is Contentry.ai different from competitors?**
> 1. **Enterprise Compliance Focus** - Built-in content moderation, policy enforcement, and approval workflows
> 2. **Strategic Profile System** - Unique 3-tier knowledge architecture (User → Company → Strategic Profiles) for context-aware content
> 3. **Full Content Lifecycle** - End-to-end from ideation to publication with analytics
> 4. **Multi-Tenant Architecture** - True enterprise isolation with SSO and advanced RBAC
> 5. **Browser Extension** - In-context content assistance wherever users write

## 1.2 B2B Use Cases

**Q5: What are the primary use cases for B2B customers?**
> 1. **Marketing Team Content Production** - Generate branded social posts, blog content, and marketing copy at scale with approval workflows
> 2. **Compliance & Moderation** - Ensure all content meets brand guidelines, regulatory requirements (financial services, healthcare), and policy standards
> 3. **Agency Client Management** - Multi-client content management with separate strategic profiles and billing per client
> 4. **Enterprise Content Governance** - Centralized control over what content gets published, who can publish, and approval chains
> 5. **Employee Advocacy Programs** - Enable employees to share approved company content on their personal profiles

**Q6: What business outcomes do B2B customers achieve?**
> - **Time Savings:** 60-80% reduction in content creation time
> - **Cost Reduction:** 40-60% reduction in content production costs vs. traditional methods
> - **Quality Improvement:** Consistent brand voice across all content
> - **Risk Reduction:** Compliance checking prevents regulatory violations
> - **Scale:** 10x content output without proportional team growth

**Q7: What's the typical ROI or value B2B customers see?**
> - "Save 15-20 hours per week on content creation"
> - "Reduce content production costs by 50%"
> - "Publish 5x more content with the same team"
> - "Zero compliance violations since implementation"
